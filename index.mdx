# LZ Kit

Independent tools for LayerZero developed by Wisdom: validate OApp config and estimate gas/fees.

**Config Validator** — Validates peers, DVNs, executor, and block confirmations across all deployment pathways. Catch misconfigs before messages get blocked or fail with NotInitializable / DVN Mismatch.

**Gas Simulator** — Estimates native gas/fees for LayerZero messages so you pass the right `msg.value` and avoid NotEnoughNative or underfunded executions.

Use them together or on their own.
